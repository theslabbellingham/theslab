ABOUT

The slab is a DIY local music paper. Our aim is to tell you about shows going on in the Bellingham area.

The physical paper is printed monthly (submit events by the 20th of the previous month for inclusion). The website will be updated more regularly.

Have a show you'd like us to add? Please fill out this form.

If you like, you can donate to help offset the costs of physical printing.

Each month we feature 2 show posters online and in print. Want us to feature yours? Fill out this form.

We're not on Instagram. Don't follow us. Just bookmark this site.
